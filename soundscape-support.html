Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a soundscape?

Tap the record button on the main screen. The app will request microphone and location permissions to mark your recording spot on the map.

Where are my recordings stored?

All recordings are stored locally on your device. They are NOT uploaded to any server or cloud storage.

How do I access premium features?

Tap the "Premium" button in the app to view and purchase premium features. The one-time purchase unlocks all advanced field recording tools.

Can I export my recordings?

Yes! Recordings can be exported from the app using the share/export function in each recording's detail view.

The app is not recording audio. What should I do?

Check that you have granted microphone permissions in iOS Settings → Soundscape Studio → Microphone.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. This will restore your premium access if you previously purchased it with the same Apple ID.
