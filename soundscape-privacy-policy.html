Privacy Policy - Soundscape Studio

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Soundscape Studio ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect precise location data (GPS coordinates) only when you use the recording feature to:

  • Mark recording spots on the map
  • Document where field recording exercises were performed

Location data is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Device Information

We collect device identifiers through Firebase Analytics for:

  • Understanding app usage patterns
  • Improving app performance and features
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics

2.3 Purchase Information

We collect purchase data through RevenueCat to:

  • Enable premium features
  • Manage in-app purchase status

This data is NOT linked to your personal identity (no account or login required).

2.4 Audio Recordings

Audio recordings you create are stored ONLY on your device in local storage (IndexedDB). We do NOT collect, transmit, or have access to your audio recordings.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Location Data: To provide core app functionality (marking recording locations)
  • Analytics Data: To improve app performance and user experience
  • Purchase Data: To manage premium feature access
  • Crash/Performance Data: To fix bugs and optimize the app

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous analytics (Google)
  • RevenueCat: For in-app purchase management

These services are used strictly for app functionality and comply with their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Audio recordings: Stored locally on your device only
  • Location data: Stored locally on your device only
  • Analytics data: Stored by Firebase (anonymized)
  • Purchase data: Managed by Apple App Store and RevenueCat

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app)
  • Opt-out of location services (via iOS Settings)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for professional and hobbyist field recording practitioners.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
