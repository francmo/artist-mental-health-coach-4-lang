Privacy Policy - Creative Wellness Music

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

Creative Wellness Music is a free wellness app for musicians. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect mood tracking data, journal entries, and exercise completion records that you voluntarily provide. All data is stored locally on your device by default.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality: mood tracking, exercise recommendations, and journaling features.

Data Storage

Local Storage: All data is stored on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers unless you explicitly enable optional cloud sync.

Optional Cloud Sync: If enabled, data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud) with encryption.

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API: Used only for AI-generated exercises when you choose to use this feature. Google's privacy policy applies.

Firebase: Used only if you enable cloud sync. Google Cloud privacy policy applies.

Your Rights

  • Access and view all your data within the app
  • Delete any or all of your data at any time
  • Disable AI features or cloud sync
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures. Local data is protected by iOS security. Cloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

No Advertising or Selling Data

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. This is a completely free app with no monetization.
