Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

In brief: Artist Mental Health Coach respects your privacy. Your personal data, journal, and uploaded artworks remain private and are never sold to third parties.

1. Who We Are

Artist Mental Health Coach is an application developed by Francesco Mariano, dedicated to the mental wellbeing of artists and creative students.

Developer: Francesco Mariano
Website: https://francmo.github.io/artist-mental-health-coach-4-lang/

2. Data Collected

We collect only the minimum data necessary for app functionality:

2.1 Authentication Data (via Firebase)

  • Email (if you choose email login)
  • Username and profile photo (if you choose Google login)
  • Anonymous user ID generated automatically

2.2 App Data

  • Mood Tracking: Your daily emotional states
  • Journal: All notes and reflections you write
  • Uploaded Artworks: Images of your artistic creations for AI analysis
  • Preferences: Language, selected theme, app settings

2.3 Data NOT Collected

  • We do not track your geographic location
  • We do not collect web browsing data
  • We do not collect contacts from your device
  • We do not use third-party cookies

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: To allow you to access your account
  • Data Saving: To sync your data across devices
  • AI Analysis: Uploaded artworks are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate feedback (images are not permanently saved by Google)
  • App Improvement: We use aggregated and anonymous statistics to improve the experience

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services Used:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and database. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: For artwork analysis. Images are processed temporarily and not saved. Google Privacy Policy
Important: Your journal and mood data remain on your device and on protected Firebase servers. Only you can access them through your account.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Data access only through user credentials
  • Secure and certified servers (Firebase/Google Cloud)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Portability: You can export your data in readable format
  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your data

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you keep your account active. When you delete your account:

  • Automatic backups are deleted within 90 days

The app is rated for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided personal data, contact us for removal.
